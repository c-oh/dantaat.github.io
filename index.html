the characters

Flint

FLINT was only 16 when the apocalypse hit. She was never one to follow the rules and dropped out of high school. Her survival roots in her loyalty, quick thinking and intuition, which make up for her lack of education.

Brent

BRENT is the calm and rational counterpart to Flint. He taught her everything she knows about fighting off the dead and doesn’t hesitate to carry out the “tough” decisions. He seems to know a little bit about everything, even if he has to make it up.
